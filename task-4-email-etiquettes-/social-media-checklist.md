## part-B
# Responsible Social Media Use: Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
- Share academic wins like project updates or forensic tips with professional captions.
- Set profiles to private and review tagged posts before they appear.
- Engage positively—comment thoughtfully on AI or study group discussions.
- Use polls/stories for batchmate feedback on tools like n8n or HackerRank.
- Follow verified accounts for career advice and VIT announcements.

## Don'ts
- Post sensitive info: no Aadhaar, address, or live locations.
- Share assignment solutions, exam snaps, or prof notes publicly.
- Argue aggressively or use slang/abuse in comments/replies.
- Accept random friend requests without mutual connections.
- Overshare personal life (e.g., late-night parties) that could impact internships.
